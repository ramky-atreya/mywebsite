About Me

I am Ramakrishnan Atreya, an economics graduate turned researcher working on power sector policy, data, and youth-focused energy literacy.

My work sits at the intersection of:

  • Energy systems & policy – especially Resource Adequacy and Demand Assessment
  • Data and storytelling – using quantitative and qualitative tools to tell evidence-based stories
  • Education and youth engagement – designing and supporting projects that help young people understand complex topics

Over the last few years I have worked as a Research Associate at FSR Global and previously as a Data Analyst at Datarivu, collaborating with journalists, philanthropy organisations, and government partners to build data-based narratives.
